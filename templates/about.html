FAQ


What does this website do?

This PTM (parent-teacher meetings) booking system is a prototype that Dunman High School could adopt to supplement the PTM booking function on DHIS (Dunman High Information System).


How do I login?

Ensure that you are signing in with your child's school email account as only email addresses ending with "dhs.sg" are allowed.


How do I book slots?

Teachers you can book slots with are automatically shown depending on your child's subject combination. Blocked slots are coloured black while free slots are coloured white.
Select your desired free slots and they will turn dark green/teal. When done, click "Update Booking" at the bottom. If successful, these selected slots will turn light green.
If you decide to cancel them, select them again and they will turn red. Click "Update Booking to finalise your cancellation.
